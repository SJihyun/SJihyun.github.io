Finding My Way

I graduated from University as an Industrial designer but I could never find a job that I was passionate about. So I started doing some freelance photography which I still really enjoy. It gave me the chance to participate in some interesting projects and I even won some awards. It became one of my passions. A few years ago I got interested in web development first as a way to showcase my photography portfolio but then as I learned more I became more interested in it. However I was always a bit afraid of committing fully to web development and changing my whole career. Last year I even started working in the course pre-requisites but I didn't make it in time. So this year I decided I would finally do it and dedicated 100% of my time to finish all pre-requisites and get enough time to build my portfolio webpage the way I imagine it. I feel that my academic formation and my photography work already give me a strong base in design concepts. The idea about my webpage was to not only showcase what I've learned about web development but also to show you a fusion of my design and photography experience.
